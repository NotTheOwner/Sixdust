    Welcome to the Sixdust website

    Take a step back from the nice calming game play of RPG's, and step out of your comfort zone with Sixdust, a 2D side-scrolling adventure game where you help people in the world, and where punches are not held back.

    This game is created by two people who have a mind for the horror of the world, and wish for more people to be aware of its problems.
